How to Play

Keyboard

Press Space or ↑ to flap upward.

Mouse

Left-click anywhere on the sky to flap.

Touch

Tap the screen to flap on phones & tablets.

Objective

Glide between the pipes without touching them or the ground.

Scoring

+1 for every pipe you clear. Speed and gaps tighten as you climb.

Coins

Collect golden coins for bonus points. Chain them for a combo multiplier.

Tips & Tricks

Small, rhythmic taps beat frantic mashing. Watch the pipe gap early and commit to a flight line before you reach it — panicking near the pipe is the #1 cause of collisions.
